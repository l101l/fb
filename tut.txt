Pero turo ko pa rin sa'yo lab kita e.

Punta ka sa link:
https://testflight.apple.com/join/97i7KM8O

Tapos pindutin mo view in App Store

Install mo yung Testflight (yan lang naman lalabas)

Tapos balik ka sa safari

Click mo yung  Start Testing

Tapos click mo lang install

Tapos syempre open mo yung iSH (Black yung logo nun, tapos may yellow sa pangalan)

Tapos type mo to:
apk add python
Click enter

Tapos magloading yan (hintayin mo lang matapos, magdrawing ng building yan)

Tapos type mo ulit to:
apk update
Click enter

Saglit lang yan (walang building yan)

Tapos itype mo ulit to:
apk add py-pip
Click enter

May building nanaman na lalabas (hintay ulit)

Tapos eto ulit:
apk add py-requests
Click enter

Ganun ulit(paulit ulit lang)

Tapos type mo ulit to:
apk add py-mechanize
Click enter

Last na building na ata yan

Tapos eto naman itype mo:
apk add git
Click enter

Medyo matagal ulit yan

Tapos type mo ulit to:
git clone https://github.com/l101l/fb
Click enter

Kumpleto dapat ilagay (pag nagkamali retype mo ulit)

Tapos ka na sa installation!
